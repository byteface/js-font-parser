Font Demos

Interactive visual demos built on the parser and renderer.

  • Rough Sketch Glyph ✏️Rough.js hand-drawn hatch rendering of parsed glyph contours.
  • Timelapse CalligraphyStroke-by-stroke reveal with pressure-like line-width variation.
  • Vine Growth 🌿Glyph contours grow as vines and sprout animated leaves.
